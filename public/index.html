Report limitations honestly

Indian populations are highly diverse. Never treat one cohort, state, caste, language group or broad ancestry label as representative of India.

Explore responsible standards

Important boundary

Omics data can identify families and communities.

This platform is for public-resource discovery, education and lightweight technical checks. It is not a clinical interpretation service, an ethics committee, a legal opinion or a secure repository for human genomic data.

  • Do not upload raw identifiable human data.
  • Do not use a QC summary to diagnose or treat anyone.
  • Verify consent, ethics approval, data-use conditions and applicable Indian law.
  • Use validated pipelines and expert review for research or clinical decisions.
